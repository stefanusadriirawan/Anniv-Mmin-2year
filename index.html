My dearest love,

Lo, there you are,

Facing this screen of yours,

As you open this letter, you know that I am here,

Always has been,

Today, my heart full with joy,

For two years have passed since our souls matched.

Two blissful revolutions 'round the sun,

Our love growing stronger with each passing day.

I vow to stand beside you,

Until time itself ceases to exist.

My very wish, my deepest desire,

Is for you to nurture my life eternally.

With boundless devotion, I declare:

My love for you shall never wore away.

You are the muse of my heart's sweet song,

The light that guides me through life's refrain.

Forever yours,

Apen
