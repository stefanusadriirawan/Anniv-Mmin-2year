My dearest love,

Lo, there you are,

Like a vision divine, illuminating my screen.

As you open this missive, know that I am here,

Ever-present, a constant in your life's tapestry.

Today, my heart swells with joy,

For two years have passed since our souls entwined.

Two blissful rotations 'round the sun,

Our love growing stronger with each passing day.

I vow to stand beside you,

Until time itself ceases to be.

My fervent wish, my deepest desire,

Is for you to grace my life eternally.

With boundless devotion, I declare:

My love for you shall never wane.

You are the muse of my heart's sweet song,

The light that guides me through life's refrain.

Forever yours,

Apen
